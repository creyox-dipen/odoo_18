How do I start creating a diagram?

It's simple! In any Odoo HTML field (like product descriptions, project notes, or emails), just type /drawio and press enter. The professional Draw.io editor will launch instantly in a fullscreen window.

Can I edit my diagrams after saving them?

Yes, absolutely! All diagrams remain fully re-editable. Just click on any existing diagram image within the Odoo editor, and it will open back up in the Draw.io canvas with all your layers and nodes preserved.

Where are my diagrams stored?

Your data security is our priority. All diagrams are stored directly as attachments within your Odoo database. We do not use any external cloud storage, ensuring your private logic and architectures stay within your own infrastructure.

Will these diagrams appear in PDF reports?

Yes! All diagrams created with this module are correctly visible and perfectly rendered in Odoo PDF reports, ensuring your printed documents maintain professional visual standards.

Is this app compatible with Odoo Online?

No, this app will not work for Odoo Online.
